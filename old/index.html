What is YAMCS

In mission control centers, a major part of the ground segment is the front-end and back-end software which is used to assist payload and system operators in their dailytasks. Here comes Yamcs (which can be understood as either ”Yet another mission control system” or as a sweet potato), a software package which has been created for long-term payload operations.
